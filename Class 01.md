> ## 1. Lists

- In Html there are three types of lists:

  * Ordered Lists: where all items in the list are numbered
  
  Example: 
  
    ` <ol>`
    
    ` <li> First Item </li>` 
       
    ` <li> Second Item </li>` 
       
    ` <li> Third Item </li>` 
        
    ` </ol>` 
    
   
    Result:
    
    1. First Item
    2. Second Item
    3. Third Item
    
    
  * Unordered Lists: whrere items begins with a bullet point
  
   Example: 
    
    ` <ul>`
    
    ` <li> First Item </li>`
    
    ` <li> Second Item </li>`
     
    ` <li> Third Item </li>`
     
    ` </ul> `
   
   
      
    Result:
    
    * First Item
   
    * Second Item
    
    * Third Item
      
  
  * Definition Lists: are made up of a set of terms along with the definitions for each of those terms.
   Example:
   
    ` <dl>`
      
    ` <dt> 1 Item </dt>`
       
    ` <dd> First Item </dd>`
        
    ` <dt> 2 Items </dt>`
        
    ` <dd> Second Item </dd>`
        
    ` <dd> Third Item </dd>`
        
    ` </dl>`

   Result:
    
    1 Item 
    
        First Item
        
    2 Items
    
        Second Item 
        
        Third Item
